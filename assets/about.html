About UVCE

University Visvesvaraya College of Engineering (UVCE) is one of India’s oldest and most respected engineering institutions.

History & Legacy

Established in 1917 by Bharat Ratna Sir M. Visvesvaraya, UVCE is the first engineering college in Karnataka and the fifth in India. With over 100 years of legacy, UVCE continues to be a symbol of excellence.

Vision & Founding

UVCE was founded to fulfill Sir Visvesvaraya’s vision of developing skilled engineers for the Mysore state, driving industrial and infrastructural development.

Programs Offered

  • B.Tech, M.Tech, PhD Programs
  • Architecture Courses (B.Arch)
  • Disciplines include: CSE, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil, IS, AI & more

Campus & Recognition

UVCE has two campuses: one at KR Circle and another at Jnana Bharathi. It is NAAC 'A' accredited, autonomous, and recognized as an Institution of State Eminence.
